About Me

I am a political scientist at the University of North Florida. I study elections, voting behavior, public opinion, and political methodology.

My research has been published in several journals including Journal of Elections, Public Opinion and Parties, Election Law Journal, Electoral Studies, American Politics Research, and Field Methods. My work has been covered by outlets such as The Washington Post , The New York Times , Vox , Newsweek , and The Conversation .

If you are interested in learning more about my research please contact me at: e.shino@unf.edu
